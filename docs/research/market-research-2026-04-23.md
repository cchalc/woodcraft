## Business Licensing (BC / White Rock)

### Municipal License
- Required for all commercial activity in White Rock
- Governed by [Business Licence Bylaw 1510](https://www.whiterockcity.ca/DocumentCenter/View/272/Consolidated---Business-License-Bylaw-1997-Number-1510-PDF)
- Apply online: [MyWhiteRock Portal](https://my.whiterockcity.ca/TempestApps/eApply/Licensing/welcome.aspx)
- Source: [White Rock Business Licences](https://www.whiterockcity.ca/901/Business-Licences-Permits)

### Trade Licensing (BC)
- Electricians: Must be licensed by [Technical Safety BC](https://www.technicalsafetybc.ca/technologies/electrical/electrical-licences)
- Plumbers: Need Certificate of Qualification
- Gas fitters: Must work for registered gas contractor
- **Dryer vent cleaners: No specific trade license required**
- Source: [TrustedPros BC Requirements](https://trustedpros.ca/license-requirements/bc)

### Referral Service Requirements
- No specific provincial regulations found for referral/intermediary services
- Standard business registration with BC Registries required
- Use [BizPaL](https://www2.gov.bc.ca/gov/content/employment-business/business/small-business/find-business-permits-licences-bizpal) to check specific requirements
